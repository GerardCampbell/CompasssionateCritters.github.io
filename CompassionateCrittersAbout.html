All About us!

At Compassionate Critters Animal Rescue, we put every animal first. We work along side government sections to help rescue and rehome the many abused, abandoned and negleced animals in the local area and beyond.

We are a local charity that have been wanting to branch out and help in the wider areas to help those animals that are in need. As we are a charity we rely on the generosity of donors, volunteers and support from the local community to carry out the good work we have been doing.

With your help and the many other kind people in this country, we hope to contiune our mission in ensuring that animals get the home they deserve!
